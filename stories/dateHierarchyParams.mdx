|Sr. No.   |  Name | Purpose   | Possible Values  |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1  | lowerLimit  | Set lower date limit on the date hierarchy | Start date in DD/MM/YYYY format.  |
| 2  | upperLimit  |Set upper date limit on date hierarchy | End date in DD/MM/YYYY format.|
| 3  | disabledList  |List of dates/months/years that are to be shown as enabled on the date hierarchy | Default: Enable all.<br/><br/>For example: ["01/01/2000","02/04/2000","09/05/2000", "8/01/2000", "11/11/2025"]<br/><br/>And disabled only those that are in the list.|
