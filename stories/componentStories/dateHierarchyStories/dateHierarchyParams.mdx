|Sr. No.   |  Name | Purpose   | Possible Values  |Mandatory|
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1  | lowerLimit  | Set lower date limit on the date hierarchy. | Start date in MM/DD/YYYY format.<br/><br/>Default: Current Year<br/><br/>Example: 12/03/2020, 02/23/2020  |No|
| 2  | upperLimit  |Set upper date limit on date hierarchy. | End date in MM/DD/YYYY format.<br/><br/>Default: Current Year + 1<br/><br/>Example: 11/12/2021, 03/12/2022|No|
| 3  | disabledList  |List of dates/months/years that are to be shown as disabled on the date hierarchy. | Default: Enable all<br/><br/>For example: ["01/01/2000","02/04/2000","w1/05/2000", "8/01/2000", "q1/2025"]|No|
| 4  | height  |Set height of date hierarchy. |Default:200px<br/><br/>For Example: 500px|No|
| 5  | showQuarters  |Show quarters in the date hierarchy. |true and false <br/><br/>Default:true |No|
| 6  | showWeeks  |Show weeks for each month in date hierarchy. |true and false <br/><br/>Default:false|No|
